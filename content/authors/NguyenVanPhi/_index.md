Phi is a Doctoral researcher at the Chair for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine at the Technical University of Munich (TUM) under MSCA doctoral training network. Previously he worked on topics of echocardiography image segmentation and cardiac motion estimation. His main research focus is the development of deep learning methods for quantitative MRI reconstruction. He is interested in the application of implicit neural representations and generative models for MRI reconstruction and data augmentation.
